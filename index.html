About Me

My name is Jessel Ann M. Piamonte, a 20-year-old residing in Casuntingan, Mandaue City. I am currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology at Cebu Technological University. Alongside my studies, I am a passionate freelance photographer and video editor, capturing moments and creating visual stories. When I'm not behind the camera, you can find me strumming my guitar, singing my heart out, or engaging in a friendly game of badminton. As I continue my educational journey, my ultimate goal is to become a skilled front-end developer. I am excited to explore the world of web development and contribute to creating captivating online experiences. Let's connect and embark on this exciting journey together!
